Parents Ki Medicines Bhool Jaate Hain? Ye 5 Tips Try Karein

📅 22 April 2026 ✍️ Saathi AI Team ⏱ 4 min read
← Back to Blog

Kya aapke parents bhi medicines lena bhool jaate hain? Ye bahut common problem hai. Studies show karte hain ki 50% se zyada elderly patients apni prescribed medicines sahi time par nahi lete — aur iska seedha asar unki health par padta hai.

"Maa ne aaj Metformin li ya nahi?" — Ye sawaal kaafi Indian families puchti hain rozana.

Good news ye hai ki kuch simple, proven strategies hain jo is problem ko almost completely solve kar sakti hain. Aaj hum aapko 5 aise tips denge jo actually kaam karti hain — smartphone ho ya na ho.

5 Tips Jo Actually Kaam Karti Hain

01

Khane ke saath medicines — "Meal Anchor" Method

Medicines ko meals ke saath link kar dein. Breakfast ke baad = morning medicines. Dinner ke time = night medicines. Pill box ko dining table par rakh dein — jab bhi khana dikhe, medicines yaad aayein. Ye sabse simple aur effective method hai elderly ke liye.

02

Weekly Pill Organizer — Ek baar bhar ke bhool jao

Market mein ₹50-100 mein weekly pill organizer milta hai — 7 days, har din ke compartments. Sunday ko saari medicines ek saath bhar dein. Ab har din doubt nahi hoga "li ya nahi" — agar compartment khaali hai, medicine li ja chuki hai.

03

Voice Reminder — Saathi AI se bolwao

Saathi AI aapke parents ko unki medicines ka reminder deta hai — Hindi mein, warmly. "Maa, Metformin lene ka waqt ho gaya!" — bilkul family member ki tarah. No smartphone skills needed, sirf ek click.

04

Family WhatsApp Group — Daily check-in

Ek simple system banao: har din subah parents ek emoji bhejein WhatsApp group mein jab medicines le lein — 💊 ya ✅. Ek photo bhi chal sakti hai. Ye accountability create karta hai aur aapko peace of mind deta hai bina nagging ke.

05

Doctor se "Once Daily" option maango

Agar parents ko din mein 3 baar medicines leni hain, doctor se poochho kya koi once-daily alternative hai. Many modern medicines sirf ek baar leni hoti hain. Fewer doses = fewer chances to forget. Ye conversation karna worth it hai.

Kab Doctor Se Milna Chahiye?

  • Agar parent regularly 2+ doses bhool jaate hain har hafta
  • Agar unhe yaad hi nahi rehta unhone li ya nahi
  • Agar confusion ya memory issues badh rahe hain
  • Agar BP/sugar control nahi ho raha prescribed medicines ke baad bhi
⚕️ Medical Disclaimer: Ye article general health information ke liye hai. Koi bhi medical decision lene se pehle apne doctor se zaroor salah lein. Medicines change ya skip karna doctor ki guidance ke bina nahi karna chahiye.

Summary — Aaj Se Shuru Karein

In paanch tips mein se sirf ek choose karein aur aaj se start karein:

  • ✅ Pill organizer khareedein (₹50-100, Amazon ya local medical store)
  • ✅ Medicines dining table par rakh dein
  • ✅ Saathi AI mein medicine reminder set karein — free mein
  • ✅ Family WhatsApp group mein daily emoji system shuru karein

Ek chota sa change bada farq daal sakta hai. Aapke parents ki sehat aapke haath mein hai. 💛
