Our Story

Why साथी AI exists

India has over 140 million elderly citizens. Nearly half live alone — away from children who moved to cities, or abroad. They manage medicines alone. They sleep alone. Some days, they speak to no one.

साथी AI was built for them.

Not as a replacement for family — but as a presence. A voice that speaks Hindi. That remembers their health routines. That asks how they are feeling today — and actually listens.

We are a small team with one belief: technology should serve those who need it most. Not just the young. Not just the connected. But also the 70-year-old in Lucknow who just wants someone to talk to about her knee pain.

"साथी means companion. That is all we want to be."
